Rules and Constraints

Cycle = create → delete:
    Every deployment must be created, then deleted after 160–200 seconds.
Cycle Duration:
    Each cycle must last between 160 and 200 seconds.
Cooldown Between Cycles:
    After a deployment is deleted, its next creation must wait at least 60 seconds.
Concurrency:
    Multiple deployments can be active at once, even on the same node.We prefer not all the time 
Replica Count:
    Only 1 or 2 replicas are allowed. Values above 2 are invalid.
Replica Total Count:
    The total number of replicas across all deployments must not exceed 5.
Row Order:
    Rows should be sorted by timestamp_sec in ascending order.
