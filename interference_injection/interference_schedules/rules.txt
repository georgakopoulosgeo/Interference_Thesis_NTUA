Rules and Constraints

Cycle = create → delete:
    Every deployment must be created, then deleted after 100–140 seconds.
Cycle Duration:
    Each cycle must last between 100 and 140 seconds.
Cooldown Between Cycles:
    After a deployment is deleted, its next creation must wait at least 40 seconds.
Concurrency:
    Multiple deployments can be active at once, even on the same node.
Replica Count:
    Only 1 or 2 replicas are allowed. Values above 2 are invalid.
Row Order:
    Rows should be sorted by timestamp_sec in ascending order.
